Tips, examples, and sources

Tips:
- Paint like a canvas, not a sketchpad. Shade each object (eg sea, river, house), leaving no white spaces
- Draw AI sees paintings as a single layer; overlapping objects may not fully display
- Draw AI operates at lower resolution vs NVIDIA's GauGAN because GPUs are disabled (to save on server costs)
